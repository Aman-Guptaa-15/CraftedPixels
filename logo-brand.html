Logo & Brand Identity

Your brand identity is the visual language that communicates who you are, what you do, and what you stand for. A well-designed logo is more than just a graphic – it’s the face of your business. At CraftedPixels, we specialize in creating strong, memorable visual identities for startups, small businesses, and creatives.

What We Offer

  • Custom Logo Design (Wordmark, Icon-based, Monogram)
  • Color Palette Selection & Styling
  • Typography & Font Pairing
  • Visual Branding Elements
  • Brand Guidelines (PDF)

Our Design Approach

  1. Discovery: We learn about your brand values, audience, and personality.
  2. Sketch & Concepts: Our designers brainstorm and sketch out multiple ideas.
  3. Refinement: We narrow it down to 2–3 strong concepts and refine them based on your feedback.
  4. Delivery: Final logo files in all required formats along with brand style guide.

Why Branding Matters

Strong branding builds trust. It tells your audience that you’re professional, reliable, and here to stay. A unified identity across all platforms — social media, website, print — helps customers recognize you instantly. That’s what we help you achieve.
