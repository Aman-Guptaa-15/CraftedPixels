Website Design

Your website is the digital face of your brand. At CraftedPixels, we build visually stunning, user-focused websites that not only look great but perform smoothly across all devices.

What We Design

  • Business Websites
  • Personal Portfolios
  • Landing Pages
  • Blogs and Content Platforms
  • Custom CMS Themes

Our Approach

  1. Discovery: We start with understanding your goals, audience, and style preferences.
  2. Wireframing: We create layout drafts to map the structure of your site.
  3. Visual Design: UI elements, typography, color palette, and imagery are brought together into a beautiful layout.
  4. Responsive Development: Your site is coded with HTML, CSS, and JS or integrated into a CMS like WordPress.
  5. Launch & Support: We help you deploy the site and stay available for tweaks or updates.

Design Principles We Follow

  • Mobile-first: Every design is responsive and optimized for mobile browsing.
  • Clarity & Simplicity: We keep content and navigation easy to understand.
  • Visual Hierarchy: We guide users through the page using contrast, size, and spacing.
  • Performance: Our pages are lightweight, fast-loading, and SEO-friendly.

Tools We Use

  • Figma, Adobe XD – For wireframing and UI design
  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript – For frontend development
  • Bootstrap – For responsive layout
  • WordPress, Webflow – For CMS-based builds

Extras We Provide

  • SEO-optimized layout & code
  • Basic on-page animations
  • Social media integrations
  • Image and icon sourcing
